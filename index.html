I am Janice... Jan... JJ...

Here's a little about me...

I am in the middle of a career transformation.
Never in my wildest thoughts that I thought I would be embarking on a journey to become a programmer.

All my life to date, I have always been told what I should do, what is the better choice to make, and even 'you brain is just not wired that way', but I never really know what I like to do.

After plentiful of soul searching and self help books, I decided that I am ready to find that one thing that sparks joy for me to do. I have always been good at what do no matter the job I held, not because I have a neck for it nor because I love it.
The one simple reason is: I refuse to be crap at what I do.

Being a parent and trying to be a decent one no small task. I am thankful that my 2 gorgeous young boys taught me a very important life lesson. It is never too late and I am free to express my passion in life. (well.. at least career wise) As they say, 'If you love what you do, you will never work a day in your life.'

So here I am, a bag of nerves and self frustration with the support of my dear family.
The journey has begun... and I feel like I am under the Northern Lights.

Special thanks to my partner in life for your continual support in releasing my inner geek that puts a smile on my face
